# Chicago Med Spa Lead Intake Prospect List

## Purpose

First outbound prospect list for the Lead Intake Command System landing page and the lead intake/follow-up automation offer.

This is a preliminary research list, not a confirmed lead pipeline. Do not create individual lead records or send outreach until a prospect is manually reviewed and selected.

## Research Assumption

- Niche: med spas, aesthetic clinics, cosmetic dermatology, and wellness clinics.
- Geography: Chicago, Illinois.
- Offer angle: lead intake and follow-up automation for consultation, appointment, quote, and booking-request workflows.
- Date researched: 2026-06-12 local user context.

## Qualification Lens

A prospect is stronger when public pages show at least three of these signals:

- visible contact form, consultation form, appointment request, scheduler, or booking CTA,
- multiple likely intake channels such as phone, form, email, text, or online scheduler,
- appointment, consultation, quote, or assessment-based service model,
- one missed lead could plausibly represent meaningful revenue,
- current intake path implies manual follow-up or callback after form submission.
