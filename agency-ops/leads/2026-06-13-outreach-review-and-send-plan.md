The drafts are generally ready for approval because they:

- Lead with a concrete observation from the prospect's public website.
- Avoid exaggerated AI claims.
- Offer a simple workflow map rather than a heavy platform rebuild.
- Ask one clear question.
- Keep the initial commitment small.

- Add a specific contact name where a public owner or operations contact is clearly available.
