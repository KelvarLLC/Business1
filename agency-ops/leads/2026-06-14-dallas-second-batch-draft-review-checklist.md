## Recommended Revision Patterns

Use these if the saved drafts need tightening.

### Landscaping Prospects

Safe phrase:

> A simple workflow map could show where new project or service inquiries enter, who sees them first, and how follow-up stays visible without changing the whole way your team works.

Avoid:

- `Your leads are falling through the cracks.`
- `AI can automate your sales process.`
- `This will make sure no lead is missed.`

### Roofing Prospects

Safe phrase:

> A lightweight intake workflow could help turn estimate requests into clearer next steps: project details, owner/office visibility, and follow-up status.

Avoid:

- `This will handle insurance claims.`
- `Automation can contact every lead for you.`
- `Your current process is too manual.`
