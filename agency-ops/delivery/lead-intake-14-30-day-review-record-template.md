# Lead Intake 14-30 Day Review Record Template

Last updated: 2026-06-14 13:02 CDT

## Purpose

Use this record 14 to 30 days after a Lead Intake Command System / Follow-Up Engine handoff. It turns early usage into a structured review of adoption, lead visibility, follow-up discipline, exceptions, support needs, proof eligibility, and sensible phase-two opportunities.

This is a reusable internal template. It is not a client commitment, support agreement, public proof item, testimonial approval, final price quote, deployment action, vendor decision, or approval to enable auto-send.

## Guardrails

- Keep this record inside the assigned repository workspace: `agency-ops/`.
- Do not store credentials, raw exports, private customer lists, regulated data, or sensitive customer messages.
- Use aggregated or anonymized examples unless the client explicitly approves named details.
- Do not publish screenshots, metrics, testimonials, case-study claims, or before/after language without specific client approval.
- Do not treat a positive review as permission to expand scope, select paid tools, change production automations, or send client-facing messages.
- Record unresolved issues clearly before recommending expansion.
