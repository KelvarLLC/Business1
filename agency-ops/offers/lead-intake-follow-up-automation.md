# Lead Intake And Follow-Up Automation Offer

## Service Summary

A focused automation package for small service businesses that need faster lead response, cleaner qualification, and fewer missed follow-ups.

## Target Client

Small local or service-based businesses that receive inquiries through forms, email, phone notes, spreadsheets, or social messages and struggle to respond consistently.

## Problem Solved

Leads often arrive in scattered places, get manually copied between tools, and depend on a busy owner or admin to remember the next step. This creates slow response times, inconsistent qualification, and lost opportunities.

## Automation Solution

Create a simple lead intake and follow-up workflow that captures new inquiries, organizes the key details, routes the lead to the right next step, and triggers timely follow-up reminders or draft responses.

## Included Scope

- Intake source review and lead-field definition.
- Lead qualification criteria and stage mapping.
- Central tracker or CRM-ready record structure.
- Follow-up reminder workflow or draft message workflow.
- Basic reporting view for new, contacted, qualified, waiting, and closed leads.
- Handoff documentation for the owner or admin team.

## Not Included By Default

- Full CRM migration.
- Paid ad management.
- Complex multi-location routing.
- Guaranteed revenue outcomes.
- Legal, compliance, or regulated-industry advice.

## Expected Business Outcome

The client gets a clearer lead pipeline, faster follow-up rhythm, and reduced manual tracking work. The practical win is fewer leads slipping through the cracks.

## Discovery Questions

1. Where do new leads come from today?
2. Who is responsible for first response?
3. What makes a lead qualified or not qualified?
4. What follow-up timing is expected?
5. What tools are already used for email, forms, spreadsheets, CRM, or scheduling?
6. What happens when a lead does not respond?
7. What reporting does the owner actually check?

## Delivery Phases

1. Map current intake and follow-up process.
2. Define lead stages, required fields, and qualification rules.
3. Build the intake-to-tracker workflow.
4. Add follow-up reminders, draft messages, or routing logic.
5. Test with sample leads and edge cases.
6. Document operating steps and handoff.

## Suggested Starting CTA

"I can map your current lead intake and follow-up process, then show you the simplest automation that would stop leads from getting missed without forcing a full CRM rebuild."
